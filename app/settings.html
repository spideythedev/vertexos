Privacy Policy

Transparency and Security Guidelines.

Effective Date: May 2026
Organization: Vertex Creative Inc.

1. Local Execution & Data Storage

Lucid OS is a client-side ecosystem. We do not collect, process, or transmit your personal data to any external servers. All your preferences (wallpapers, username, high scores, generated code) are stored securely on your local device using the browser's standard localStorage API.

2. Hardware Telemetry

The "System Specifications" tab reads data directly from your browser's navigator object to display your CPU cores, RAM, and screen resolution. This data is exclusively read-only for display purposes. It is never logged or sent over the network.

3. No Tracking or Analytics

Lucid OS contains absolutely zero tracking pixels, analytics engines (such as Google Analytics), or third-party cookies. Your usage, application time, and productivity metrics remain entirely private to your machine.

4. Network Requests

The only external network requests made by Lucid OS are to load foundational styling frameworks (Tailwind CSS via CDN) and icon libraries (Lucide Icons). No identifying telemetry is attached to these requests by our software.
